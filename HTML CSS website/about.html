About Hampus Olsson

Founder of Spacetofu in Hong Kong and co-founder of GrandWorks in India. Olsson has been making websites and pixels since he was 8 years old. Living in Goa, India - Where the mighty monkeys and holy cows live.

For the last five years, Olsson has been featured and participating in the yearly art exhibition called Desktopography, and for the last couple of years, and he's also volunteered to create the official Paranoid Android Wallpapers.

"I strive for pixel-perfection and have an extreme passion for what I do."


"I love the word "create". Back in 1997 when I created my first website, that word inspired me. I was 8 years old at the time. My dream was to continue creating and learn to make more things, to evolve my creativity and being happy at the same time.
